## NeoCam

NeoCam is an edge-cloud platform for real-time, non-invasive neonatal monitoring in intensive care units. The system combined on-device computer vision and cloud services to infer limb motion, pain-related signals, and breathing rate from multimodal data.

The project won first place in Europe and second place globally in the 2021 OpenCV AI Competition. It also led to a publication in IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (2023).

## COVID-19 Impact of NPIs

This work analyzed non-pharmaceutical interventions (NPIs) in Spain during the second COVID-19 wave. A daily restriction-intensity index was built from provincial and municipal policy data and linked to transmission metrics.

Results showed that a 34% increase in overall restriction intensity was associated with a 22% reduction in transmission after one week. The study was published in Frontiers in Public Health (2023).

## SNOMED-CT Coding for Pathology Reports

This line of work focused on natural language processing support for structured clinical coding in hospital reports, with the objective of improving consistency and retrieval of pathology information.
