description: Machine learning for tuna biomass estimation from echosounder and oceanographic data.

Tun-AI estimates tuna presence and biomass from echosounder buoy signals combined with oceanographic variables and historical catch records.

The model was deployed at commercial scale and is reported to be used by more than 1,500 vessels. The project produced publications in Fisheries Research (2022) and Marine Ecology Progress Series (2023).
