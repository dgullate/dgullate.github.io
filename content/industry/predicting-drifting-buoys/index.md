---
title: Predicting Drifting Buoys
start_date: '2019-04-01'
end_date: '2019-07-01'
description: Lagrangian forecasting of drifting FAD trajectories using ocean-current models.
category: maritime
---

In collaboration with Satlink, this project modeled the drift trajectories of fish aggregating devices (FADs) to reduce buoy loss near coastlines and improve deployment planning. The work used daily positions from more than 40,000 drifting buoys and ocean-current forecasts from Copernicus and HYCOM.

We developed a Lagrangian integration framework to forecast drift and used the proprietary buoy dataset to validate ocean circulation models. The work produced a publication in ICCS 2019 on validating ocean general circulation models with drifting buoy data.
