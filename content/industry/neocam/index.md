NeoCam is an edge-cloud platform for real-time, non-invasive neonatal monitoring in intensive care units. The system combines on-device computer vision and cloud services to infer limb motion, pain-related signals, and breathing rate from multimodal data.

The project won first place in Europe and second place globally in the 2021 OpenCV AI Competition. It also led to a publication in IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (2023).
